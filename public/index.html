App Definition

You can keep your polls and come back later to access them. You can share your polls with your friends. You can see the aggregate results of your polls. You can create a poll with a title, a question, and at most 10 options. You can delete polls that you decide you don't want anymore. You can see and vote on everyone's polls. You can see the results of polls in chart form.
